Tricks that I would love to know how to do someday

    • Air Tricks
      1. Ollie: A simple trick where the boarder pops the snowboard by digging into the tail and then up into the air.
      2. Nollie: A nose ollie that is similar to the traditional ollie but performed off the nose of the snowboard.
      3. Switch Ollie: An ollie performed while riding switch.
      4. Backflip: The rider performs a backflip after gaining air over a jump.
      5. Front Flip: The rider flips forward.
      6. Tamedog: A cartwheel-style flip.
    • Grab Tricks
      1. One-Two: The rider grabs the heel edge of the snowboard using their front hand and reaching behind their back foot.
      2. Frontside Grab: The rider grabs the toe edge with the back hand.
      3. Melon: The riderâ€™s lead hand goes behind the lead leg to grab the heel edge of the snowboard, with the front leg boned.
      4. Method: The knees bend as the snowboard is lifted behind the back, with the lead hand grabbing the heel edge.
    • Rail Tricks
      1. 50-50: A rail trick where the rider balances on the snowboard with equal weight on both feet while sliding down a rail.
      2. Tail Press: A press trick where the rider presses the tail of the snowboard while keeping the nose in the air.
    • Jump Tricks
      1. Frontside 180: A jump trick where the rider rotates 180 degrees in the air while facing the direction they came from.
      2. Indy Grab: A grab trick where the rider grabs the toe edge of the snowboard between their bindings with their back hand.
